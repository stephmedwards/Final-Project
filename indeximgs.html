Instagram worthy swimming spots.

  1. Head to Hampstead Heath Swimming Ponds for a picturesque swim
  2. Get a few lengths in at the London Fields Lido
  3. For a bit of luxury, check out this rooftop pool at The Bekley.

Yoga in the clouds.

  1. Sun rise Yoga at Sky Gardens to kick start your day
  2. Sretch yourself out with a view of the City at Coq d'Argent

Get your cardio fix.

  1. Jog in company, enter a Park Run
  2. Get your heart racing with Barry at Barry's Bootcamp
  3. Throw some weights in with a good sprint at 1Rebel

Organic goodness (but with flavour).

  1. For some posh healthy nosh head west to Daylesford. Get the buttermilk scotch pancakes.
  2. Get your tie-die prints out for this vegan spot in Bethnal Green. Great food, relaxed atmosphere and fairy lights make the Gallery Cafe a winner
  3. Good life eatery will make you feel whole again with its superfood salads and smoothies.

Make the most important meal the best.

  1. The porridge and topping combinations at 26 Grains in Neals Yard, Covent Garden are to to-die-for.
  2. Dine at Ethos for some delicios eggs any and every style
  3. Head east to Raw Duck for some buckwheat pancakes, smoked bacon and maple syrup. Yumm

Sometimes you just need some meat...

  1. Go to Ottelenghis for healthy but hearty food with a good selection of meats and salads.
  2. Healthy protein packed food delivered to your door. Check out the Mae Deli brought to us by healthy blogger Deliciously Ela.
  3. For when you're feeling a bit naughty, head to Blues Kitchen for some southern style cookin'.
